# Demo Recording Checklist

Upload a private demo video and send us the link. Use this checklist to make sure the video covers the expected flow.

## Required

- Show the Client Database page.
- Show at least one functional interaction: search, filter, row selection, sorting, editing, or similar.
- Show the Intake New Customer flow.
- Simulate or implement agreement upload/manual entry.
- Show extracted or entered fields.
- Confirm the new client and show it in the database.
- Show a Sales change request through a chat or chat-like agent interface.
- Show the agent clarifying or summarizing the request.
- Show the proposed change in UAT/staging.
- Show Sales approval.
- Show the change promoted to production.

## Bonus

- Show rollback from a bad production change.
- Show an audit trail.
- Show tests, checks, or validation before promotion.

## Good Demo Habits

- Keep the recording between 5 and 8 minutes.
- Narrate the user problem and the workflow.
- Mention where you simulated external systems.
- Mention one or two tradeoffs you made because of the timebox.
- Do not spend the whole demo on code. Show the product working.
